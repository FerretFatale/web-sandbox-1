Launch Checklist

Mandatory gates for publishing updates to the Feature Deep Dives / Showcase area.

Content gate

  • Gate C1: Every page includes signal, mechanism, evidence, and boundary cues.
  • Gate C2: No sales or monetization language appears in hero, body, or closeout copy.
  • Gate C3: Claims are evidence-backed and scoped to demonstrable outcomes.

Experience gate

  • Gate E1: Navigation links are stable from Public Guide to Showcase pages.
  • Gate E2: Responsive behavior remains readable on mobile and desktop.
  • Gate E3: Visual hierarchy preserves quick scan and deep-read paths.

Safety gate

  • Gate S1: No private credentials, runtime secrets, or sensitive records exposed.
  • Gate S2: Public/private boundary statements are present and clear.

Verification gate

  • Gate V1: Run manual split and publisher tests before closure.
  • Gate V2: Record gatekeeper post-review and closure evidence artifacts.
