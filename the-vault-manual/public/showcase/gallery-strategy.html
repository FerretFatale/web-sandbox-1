Gallery Strategy

Use a three-beat story: problem (fragmented life tooling), system response (The Vault orchestration), proof (repeatable outcomes + governance).

Audience tiers

  1. Curious visitor: explain clearly in under 90 seconds.
  2. Technical collaborator: provide architecture and governance links.
  3. Potential partner: show reliability, privacy, and workflow maturity.

Section order

  1. Hero narrative
  2. Capability blocks
  3. Case-study cards
  4. Safety/governance summary
  5. Next-step CTA
