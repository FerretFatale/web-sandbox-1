Information Architecture

The showcase IA is designed to let visitors escalate from quick confidence to deep technical trust without confusion.

Primary journey

  1. Public Guide introduces mission and trust posture.
  2. Showcase Hub frames the deep-dive method (signal, mechanism, evidence, boundary).
  3. Deep-dive pages provide focused proof and concrete process details.
  4. Case Studies close the loop by tying decisions to measured outcomes.

Hierarchy choices

  • High-level narrative first, technical depth second.
  • Repeated visual and textual patterns for fast scanning.
  • Direct path back to Public Guide and Manual Hub from every key page.

Anti-friction rules

  • Stable filenames and deterministic links.
  • No hidden gates for reading core proof material.
  • No sales detours or conversion funnel copy.

Boundary handling in IA

Public pages show outcomes, architecture patterns, and quality behavior. Private runtime data, credentials, and sensitive operator records remain outside this topology by design.
