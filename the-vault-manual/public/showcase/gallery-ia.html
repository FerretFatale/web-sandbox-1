Public Showcase · IA

Information architecture that makes the next click obvious

The showcase should never trap a visitor in a dead end. Its job is to guide a quick skim toward evidence, while always preserving an easy return path to the Public Guide and the showcase hub.

Primary journey

1
Public Guide

Introduces the system in plain language and sets expectations.

2
Showcase Hub

Presents the proof cards and the navigation map.

3
Gallery Pages

Explain narrative, structure, and presentation choices.

4
Case Studies

Tie the story back to concrete outcomes and evidence.

IA rules

  • Every page must have a clear way back to the hub and the Public Guide.
  • The showcase should group exhibit pages by purpose, not by implementation accident.
  • There are no hidden pages or placeholder paths in the public narrative.
