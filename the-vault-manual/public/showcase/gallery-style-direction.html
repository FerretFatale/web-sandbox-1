Gallery Style Direction

Visual principles

  • Warm editorial tone (not sterile SaaS style).
  • Clear hierarchy with strong headings and short narrative blocks.
  • Cards for capabilities and case studies; avoid dense paragraphs.

Accessibility baseline

  • Readable color contrast for text and links.
  • Single-column fallback for mobile widths.
  • Explicit link labels and predictable back-navigation.

Motion and interaction

  • Minimal motion; prioritize clarity and load speed.
  • Hover states are optional enhancements, never required for comprehension.
