Case Study Templates

Use this format for every showcase proof card.

Template

  1. Context: what real problem existed.
  2. Workflow: which personas/tools were used.
  3. Constraints: approvals, privacy, or safety gates involved.
  4. Outcome: measurable result achieved.
  5. Reuse path: how this becomes repeatable in future lanes.

Suggested first three case studies

  • Manual governance split and publish safety hardening.
  • Mission planning with TaskMaster + consensus/governance checkpoints.
  • Email/operations lane with strict no-private-data publishing boundary.
