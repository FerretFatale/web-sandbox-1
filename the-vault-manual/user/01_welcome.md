# Welcome - What is The_Vault?

The_Vault is a local-first personal AI operating system. It coordinates specialized personas to turn ideas, tasks, and decisions into structured outcomes with explicit safety gates.

## What makes it different

1. Persona specialization instead of one generic assistant.
2. Mission workflow for multi-step work.
3. Approval gates for risky or external actions.
4. Local authority first: your files and governance are primary.
5. Repeatable operations with logs, tests, and documented runbooks.

## What The_Vault can do for you

1. Organize and execute tasks across life and business domains.
2. Track recurring routines and scheduled priorities.
3. Support research, planning, writing, and operations.
4. Manage proposal and approval flows before live-impact actions.
